Control and monitor your home from anywhere in the world. MySmartHomes Companion lets you control all your devices and provides advanced location and notification engines to allow for complex mobile automations.

MySmartHomes is an open-source home automation platform that focuses on local control and privacy. Supported products include:

MySmartHomes Companion requires an iPhone running iOS 10.3 or later and a configured MySmartHomes instance. The instance must use MySmartHomes Cloud or be accessible from the Internet and secured with a valid, non self-signed, SSL certificate for location tracking. Basic notifications work without external access.
