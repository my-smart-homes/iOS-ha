Control and monitor your home from anywhere in the world. MySmartHomes Companion lets you control all your devices and provides advanced location and notification engines to allow for complex mobile automations.

MySmartHomes is an open-source home automation platform running on Python 3. Supported products include:

- Amazon Echo (Alexa)
- Apple TV
- Arduino
- Belkin WeMo

- ecobee

- Plex
- Pushbullet
- Sonos
- Wink
- Z-Wave

MySmartHomes Companion requires an iPhone running iOS 9.3 or later and a previously configured MySmartHomes instance. The instance must be accessible from the Internet and secured with a valid, non self-signed, SSL certificate for location tracking. Basic notifications work without external access.
